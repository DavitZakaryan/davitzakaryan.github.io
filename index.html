FAQ

How much does a website cost for a small business in Glendale?

A landing page (one-page website) starts at $750, which includes design, mobile-friendly build, basic SEO setup, and domain + hosting for the first year. A full business website with 3–5 pages starts at $1,500. You can get an exact quote in 60 seconds using the calculator on this page.

How long does it take to build a website?

A landing page is typically delivered in 5–7 business days. A multi-page business website takes 10–14 days. Rush delivery (3 days) is available for an additional 25%. All projects include 2 rounds of revisions at no extra charge.

Do you work with Russian and Armenian-speaking businesses?

Yes. I speak English, Russian, and Armenian — I work with the Glendale and Los Angeles community in whichever language is most comfortable. I can also build your website in multiple languages so your customers can read it in their native language.

What does the Local SEO package include?

The Local SEO package ($350) includes setting up and optimizing your Yelp, Apple Maps, and Bing listings, on-site keyword optimization, and schema markup. Combined with the Google Business Profile setup ($250), it covers everywhere your local customers search for businesses like yours.

Will I own my website after it's built?

Yes — 100%. You own the domain, the hosting account, and all the files. There is no lock-in. If you ever want to move to a different provider or take over the site yourself, everything is yours. No hostage situations.

Do you work with businesses outside of Glendale?

Absolutely. I serve the entire Los Angeles area, including Burbank, Pasadena, Eagle Rock, Silver Lake, and beyond. Most of the work is done remotely, so location is not a barrier — though I'm always happy to meet in person in the Glendale area.
